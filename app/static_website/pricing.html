Common Questions

What kind of data sources does the AI use?

Our AI leverages a vast array of publicly available data, including market reports, news articles, financial filings, company websites, academic papers, industry publications, government data, social media sentiment (where applicable), and more. The system prioritizes reputable sources and cross-references information for accuracy. The underlying `open-deep-research` tool orchestrates the data gathering based on your query.

How specific can my research request be?

Extremely specific! The more detail you provide in the "Research Topic/Details" field on the order form, the more targeted and valuable your report will be. Ask direct questions, define specific competitors or market segments, request analysis for particular timeframes or regions. Our AI thrives on clear parameters.

Is my research request and data kept confidential?

Yes, 100%. Confidentiality is paramount. Your research request, company details, and the generated report are treated as highly sensitive information and are protected by robust security measures. We do not share or resell your data.

What is the typical length or format of the report?

Reports are typically delivered as comprehensive PDF documents. Length varies based on the complexity of the request and the report tier (Premium reports are generally more extensive). They include executive summaries, key findings, detailed analysis sections, data visualizations (charts/graphs where applicable), and source references. Premium reports may include raw data exports.

What if I need revisions or have follow-up questions?

Due to the automated, high-speed nature of the AI generation, we currently do not offer iterative revisions. However, the Premium Deep Dive includes more extensive analysis designed to anticipate follow-up needs. For truly complex, ongoing research partnerships, please contact us to discuss custom enterprise solutions.

How does the payment process work?

We partner with Lemon Squeezy for secure payment processing. After submitting your order details, you'll be redirected to their secure checkout page to complete your payment via credit card or other supported methods. Acumenis never handles or stores your full payment information.
