Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Nexus Plan ("Nexus Plan," "we," "us," or "our"). We provide an autonomous AI engine for deep market analysis and competitor intelligence (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website (the "Site") and use our Service. Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the site or use the service.

We reserve the right to make changes to this Privacy Policy at any time and for any reason. We will alert you about any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date of this Privacy Policy. You are encouraged to periodically review this Privacy Policy to stay informed of updates.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect information about you in a variety of ways. The information we may collect via the Site and Service includes:

A. Personal Data You Provide

When you initiate an analysis or contact us, you may voluntarily provide us with certain personally identifiable information, such as:

  • Contact Information: Your name and email address when you contact support or potentially during the order process.
  • Analysis Objectives: The specific details, parameters, company names, market segments, competitors, and questions you provide for the AI to analyze ("Query Data"). While this may contain business information, we treat it with the utmost confidentiality as outlined below.

B. Payment Data

All financial transactions are processed through our third-party payment processor, Lemon Squeezy. When you make a purchase, you provide your payment information directly to Lemon Squeezy. We do not collect or store your full credit card number or other sensitive payment details. We may receive transaction confirmation data from Lemon Squeezy (such as purchase amount and date) but not your full financial details.

C. Derivative Data (Website Usage)

Information our servers automatically collect when you access the Site, such as your IP address, browser type, operating system, access times, and the pages you have viewed directly before and after accessing the Site. We may use standard analytics tools (like Plausible Analytics or a self-hosted equivalent, prioritizing privacy) to gather this aggregated, anonymized data to improve website performance and user experience. We do not typically use cookies that track personal information across sites.

3. How We Use Your Information

Having accurate information permits us to provide you with a smooth, efficient, and customized experience. Specifically, we may use information collected about you via the Site or Service to:

  • Fulfill your analysis request and generate the intelligence report.
  • Process payments and manage your order.
  • Respond to your customer service requests and support needs via email (support@Nexus Plan.ai).
  • Monitor and analyze usage and trends to improve the Site and Service functionality.
  • Maintain the security and integrity of our systems.
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations.

Crucially, your specific Query Data is used *solely* for the purpose of generating the requested analysis report for you. It is not used to train general AI models beyond the ephemeral context of your request, nor is it shared or sold to third parties.

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, trade, rent, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information or confidential Query Data to outside parties except as described below:

  • Service Providers: We utilize secure, third-party services for essential functions:
    • Payment Processing: Lemon Squeezy handles payments securely. Their privacy policy governs their data use.
    • AI Model APIs: We access large language models (e.g., via OpenRouter) through secure APIs. These providers process your Query Data ephemerally to generate the synthesis but are contractually obligated (and technically designed) not to retain or use this specific data for their own model training post-request.
    • Infrastructure: Cloud hosting providers (e.g., AWS, Vercel) host our Site and backend systems under strict security protocols. They do not have access to the content of your Query Data or reports.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., a court or government agency), but only to the extent legally mandated and after careful review.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your information might be transferred as part of the business assets, subject to the commitments made in this Privacy Policy.

5. Data Security

We implement robust administrative, technical, and physical security measures designed to protect your information. Your Query Data and generated reports are processed in isolated environments and stored encrypted at rest. Access is strictly limited on a need-to-know basis. While we have taken reasonable steps to secure the information you provide to us, please be aware that despite our efforts, no security measures are perfect or impenetrable, and no method of data transmission can be guaranteed against interception or misuse.

6. Data Retention

We retain your Query Data and the generated report only as long as necessary to fulfill the analysis request and provide customer support related to that specific order, typically for a limited period (e.g., 30-90 days) unless required otherwise by law. Contact information provided via support emails may be retained longer as part of standard business communication records. Aggregated, anonymized website analytics data may be kept longer for trend analysis.

7. Your Rights (GDPR/CCPA Considerations)

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information. These may include:

  • Right to Access: Requesting copies of the personal information we hold about you (primarily contact information).
  • Right to Rectification: Requesting correction of inaccurate contact information.
  • Right to Erasure ('Right to be Forgotten'): Requesting deletion of your contact information and any associated Query Data/reports, subject to legal retention requirements.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Requesting limitations on how we use your contact information.
  • Right to Object: Objecting to certain processing activities.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at support@Nexus Plan.ai. We will respond to verifiable requests within the timeframes mandated by applicable law.

Note: Due to the autonomous and ephemeral nature of the AI analysis process using third-party models, retrieving or modifying Query Data *after* an analysis has begun or completed may be technically infeasible.

8. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We primarily use essential session information necessary for the basic functioning of the Site and order process. We aim to minimize the use of persistent tracking cookies. Any analytics tools used are configured to respect user privacy and anonymize data where possible (e.g., IP anonymization). You can typically manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

9. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If we become aware that a child under 18 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information.

10. International Transfers

Your information, including personal data, may be transferred to — and maintained on — computers located outside of your state, province, country, or other governmental jurisdiction where the data protection laws may differ. We utilize service providers (like cloud hosting and AI APIs) that may operate globally, but we select providers committed to high standards of data protection and security, often relying on mechanisms like Standard Contractual Clauses for transfers where applicable.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be indicated by an updated "Last Updated" date and will be effective as soon as it is accessible. We encourage you to review this policy frequently to be informed of how we are protecting your information.
